Our Services

1. Loans

Discover affordable loan solutions tailored to meet your financial needs. We offer a variety of loan options to suit different repayment schedules and preferences, ensuring flexibility and reliability..

Daily Loans

Interest Rate: 20%

Requirements:

  • 10% loan security (collateral).
  • Recommendation letter from the LC1 chairman of your working area.
  • A guarantor.

Weekly Loans

Interest Rate: 17%

Requirements:

  • 15% loan security & business assets (fixed) as collateral.
  • Recommendation letter from the LC1 chairman of your working area.
  • A guarantor.

Monthly Loans

Interest Rate: 15%

Requirements:

  • Land title, agreement, car, or buildings as collateral.
  • Recommendation letter from the LC1 chairman of your residence.

2. Savings

Secure and flexible savings plans designed to help you grow your wealth with peace of mind.

Savings Plan Details

Our savings plans are tailored to ensure flexibility while offering competitive returns on your investments.

Interest Rates and Conditions:
  • At **maturity** (when the client has invested for a full year), the total investment earns 20% simple interest.
  • Clients can request to withdraw their investments at any point as long as they have invested for at least 6 months.
  • Investments withdrawn before maturity (less than 1 year) earn no interest.
